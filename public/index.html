Who we are

We're Center for Rights (the 501c3 sister of Fight for the Future); we're the new Internet Freedomadvocacy group that organized last year's massive online protest to stop SOPA, the Internet censorship bill.

Why we made this

Center for Rights (link), alongside its 501c4 sister organization Fight for the Future (link), helped plan the SOPA blackout.
