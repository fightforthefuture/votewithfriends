Make it easy.

We're building the simplest way to register to vote (based on Turbovote) with built-in support for online registration where available.

Check out our widget
down

Go viral.

Every voter we reach will join the “Vote with Friends” Facebook app that lets them:
  • Invite their friends to register and vote
  • Track which friends haven't registered or pledged yet
  • Bring voters to the polls and make sure their friends vote on election day.
  • Create and join “voting blocks” for specific issues.
  • Learn more about candidates and causes they care about
down

Rally supporters.

Voters can start and join voting blocks for the issues, sites, and groups they care about, and gather massive followings.

Set up Voting Blocks (coming soon)

Get. Out. The. Vote..

Every voter will have a common mission to make sure everyone votes. This includes:
  • Automated reminders about the deadlines and requirements for voting.
  • Reminders from friends.
  • Reminders to vote early (where avail.)
  • Ballots mailed to your house (where avail.)
down

Mobilize millions to vote.

In the days leading up to election day, the coalition of sites that acts on September 25th's National Voter Registration Day (all of them, we hope, and many more) will activate again to rally even more users.

down

No broken promises.

On election day, we'll give you a live list of your friends who haven't voted yet, so you can track them down and get them to vote.

Who we are

We're Center for Rights; we're the new Internet Freedom advocacy group that organized last year's massive online protest to stop SOPA, the Internet censorship bill.

Want to get in touch? Email us at team@fightforthefuture.org

Why we made this

Center for Rights, alongside its 501c4 sister organization Fight for the Future, helped plan the SOPA blackout. Now, we're using the same tools (and some new ones) to help internet users get engaged with democracy like never before -- having an opportunity to do that is too good to miss!
